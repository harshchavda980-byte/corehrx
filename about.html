About CoreHRX

We are a next-generation HR technology company built to simplify, automate, and enhance every part of the employee lifecycle.

Who We Are

CoreHRX Pvt. Ltd., founded by Harsh Chavda, is an HR technology company based in Ahmedabad, India. Our mission is to bring modern, intelligent, and compliant HR tools to businesses of all sizes. We focus on building software that feels simple — but solves complex HR problems.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and innovative HR solutions provider — delivering seamless digital experiences for companies, HR teams, and employees.

Our Mission

Design HR technology that helps companies focus on what matters most: their people. We aim to automate repetitive tasks, ensure compliance, and create a delightful employee journey from onboarding to exit.

What We Believe

✔ Technology should simplify, not complicate.
✔ HR deserves beautiful, modern tools.
✔ Every company — small or large — should have access to powerful HR automation.
✔ Employee experience is the new competitive advantage.
